Design engineering is not just translating mockups into code. It helps teams test product decisions while the idea is still flexible.

## What matters

- Turn the key flow into something people can click and react to.
- Reuse design-system constraints instead of starting from zero.
- Test with realistic data, states, and edge cases.

When the prototype feels close to the final product, feedback becomes more specific and risks surface earlier.
