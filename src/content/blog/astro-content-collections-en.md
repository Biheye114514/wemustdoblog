Personal websites often become small content systems over time. Blog posts need dates, tags, and descriptions. Projects need roles, links, and technology notes.

Content Collections make these fields explicit. Pages get type hints, and missing data can be caught during builds.

## Good fits

- Blog posts and long-term notes
- Project case studies
- Resource lists
- Personal documentation

For student sites and small portfolios, this is simple enough to maintain and structured enough to grow.
